Structure of the project

Project Garden Gnome is a simplistic web app made with Django and Python. Using the python-weather module, we provide information that can help people with green thumbs take better care of their plants!

What it does?

Get live weather information and know whether the plant you may wish to grow is having a good time with the weather or not! Also, get tips to help your green friend be in a better mood.
